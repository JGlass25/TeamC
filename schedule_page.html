Course Input Page

How to input courses:
1. Login to the nest
2. Click "Student+" under "Administrative Services"
3. Click "Student Profile"
4. Under "Curriculum and Courses" click "Student Services"
5.Select the term you wish to pull courses from
6. To the right of "Schedule", click "Schedule Details"
7. Highlight, copy and paste ALL text starting from the last number in CRN up to the down arrow next to the course name.(Use this image as a reference)
8. Paste the text into the text box below.
Repeat steps 7 and 8 for all courses to view your whole schedule.
